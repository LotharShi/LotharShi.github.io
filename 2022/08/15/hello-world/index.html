About the past…

I remember that I used to have a blog when I was in my second year of University. I rented a cloud server, installed WordPress on it, wrote a few posts about some practice and even decorated with some small open-source components. Oh, I even bought a domain name for it.

It should be exactly the February of 2020, because of the COVID I had to stay at home and was a little bit boring. So perhaps I did the blog things simply for the feeling of freshness.

And obviously, the small amount of posts at that time were the only ones. I didn’t persist to writing so the blog was totally abandoned.

A new start NOW!

After I finished my Bachler’s and started to thriving for the Master’s, I began to realize what a blog could possibly means to me.

It doesn’t matter if nobody ever come to see my posts or try to learn something from them. It could just be some of the record of my growth, like how I come along with the learning, and how I tried to solve various kinds of difficulties I meet.

Writing could also be a way to help me better understand and memorize knowledge.

So I am back, buidling this website and start to do some post writing about the either meaningful or simply just interesting things I met.

Will my blog go further this time?

Hmmm… it’s hard to say. But at least I will try to make it so.
